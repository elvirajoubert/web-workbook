About Us

We have more than 17 years experience in Pet Grooming Excellence. . To earn the title of Trusted Expert, our employees have all passed very rigorous written and hands-on training. “Grooms By Leah“ PET GROOMING gives you: modern equipment, state-of-the-art procedures and a personal touch. We know how much these contribute to you and your pet. Prior to your pet’s grooming session, we perform a complete and detailed exam. We want to make certain that your pet gets the best and proper care. All of our staff are experts at gentle, confident animal care and handling. We are also highly skilled groomers who regularly deliver exceptionally beautiful results. Our goal is always to make your pet look and feel fabulous. Our mission is to set a new standard in pet health care. We are passionate about making pet care more affordable, convenient and accessible. Pets are a significant part of our families and their health and happiness is paramount to us.

The team at Figi's of North Austin is dedicated to providing our four-legged guests with a safe and fun environment. We ask that you bring your dog to our location prior to their stay so our team can meet your dog and get to know them. This will help us determine whether our open play environment is the right fit for them. We also require that all daycare and boarding dogs are current on their vaccinations. We don’t want you to worry about illness or aggressive dogs, which is why our staff is trained and certified in dog body language and in socializing dogs. In addition, we never admit a dog with fleas or communicable diseases. Please remember to bring any medication your dog may be taking presently.
